Project Title : Again

Overview Of this Project

We have observed students do not ask questions in the class thinking it will make them look like they are not that intelligent than others.

so they stop themselves from asking questions.

And they dont get their doubt clarified

And they go on youtube or other platforms to learn the subject before the exams and score a average mark in their exams

so by the end of the semester they are not learning the subject instead they are just knowing the subject to score the marks

And in the mean time from start of semester to end of semester they are entangled to only academic syllabus and they find hard time to learn a skill which will help them in their career

so something has to be done

so the first problem to tackle was to hide the identity of the learner and motivate and make him/her to ask questions

So we Designed this idea of Anonymous learning(gain)

What does this webapplication do or provide?

- The academic content is structured by the professors in a way that what they tought in the college will be uploaded it over here

- And student is provided with the credentials and he can access the lectures

- And if at a particular lecture video he gets a doubt at the same lecture video below we have provided anonymous question box where he can post his doubts and get clarified by students replying to it or the professor
